UOGA Outfitter Review Checklist Template

This is the field guide for the CSV checklist template. Use the CSV for data entry and this page as the reviewer reference.

Possible Outcomes

  • Hold / Do Not Publish
  • Vetted
  • C.P.O.

Minimum For Vetted

  • Identity substantially complete
  • Operational profile substantially complete
  • No blocking conflict
  • Suitable for publication as a trusted resource

Additional For C.P.O.

  • All Vetted criteria met
  • Higher professional completeness
  • Stronger consistency and responsiveness
  • No unresolved material concern

Checklist Sections

  • Identity
  • Operational Profile
  • Registration / Licensing
  • Authority / Access Claims
  • Professional Completeness
  • Risk / Conflict Check
  • Decision Fields

Decision Fields In CSV

  • reviewOutcome
  • publish
  • eligibleForVetted
  • eligibleForCpo
  • needsFollowUp
  • reviewerNotes
  • reviewedBy
  • reviewedDate
Note: Keep the CSV as the source-of-truth working file. This HTML page is just a readable reference for the same checklist structure.
