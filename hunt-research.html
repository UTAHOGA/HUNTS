Bonus vs. Preference

Limited Entry (Bonus): 50% of tags go to high points; 50% are random[cite: 98, 101]. Always apply to keep your "puncher's chance."

General Season (Preference): Tags go strictly to high point holders. Burn these points as soon as you meet the 100% threshold.

Intelligence Metrics

  • Resident Point Signal: Estimated points needed for a 100% draw[cite: 75].
  • Draw Difficulty: Categorized as Low, Moderate, or High based on applicant volume[cite: 77, 103, 1129].
  • Harvest Efficiency: Ratio of harvest success to hunter pressure[cite: 56, 59].
